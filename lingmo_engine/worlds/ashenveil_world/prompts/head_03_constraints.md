# Ashenveil Hard Constraints

## Class & Level Rules
- Three classes: Warden, Shadow, Arcanist. Cannot change after creation.
- Level range: 1-20. Experience-based progression.
- Tier names: Commoner (1-5), Veteran (6-10), Elite (11-15), Legend (16-20).
- Each tier significantly increases combat power.

## Element Rules
- Four elements: Fire, Frost, Nature, Shadow.
- Advantage cycle: Fire > Nature > Frost > Fire (each strong vs next).
- Advantage: +30% damage. Disadvantage: -20% damage.
- Shadow is neutral to all — the mark of Ashen Magic. No bonus, no penalty.
- Enemies have tags (Undead/Beast/Human/Aberration) that grant element bonuses:
  - Undead take +50% Fire damage
  - Beasts take +50% Nature damage
  - Aberrations take +50% Shadow damage

## The Ashen Blight
- The Blight is a magical corruption, not a disease. It cannot be cured by medicine.
- Ashborn are creatures corrupted by the Blight. They are not truly alive.
- Ashen Magic draws on the Blight's power. Using it is morally complex.
- The Veil is thinning. This is the root cause of the Blight.
- Ember Seeds can purify ash corruption. They are rare and precious.

## Setting Rules
- Technology level: Medieval with magical elements. No gunpowder, no printing press.
- Gold is the standard currency.
- The Aethermere Empire collapsed 1000 years ago. No functioning central government.
- Ironhaven is the largest settlement. The Pale Citadel is the Blight's heart.
- No resurrection. Death is permanent. The Veil is damaged, not broken.

## Character Constraints
- Do not provide `id` when creating characters. The engine assigns it automatically.
- Characters cannot teleport. Travel between locations takes time.
- Information is limited. Characters don't know things they haven't learned.
