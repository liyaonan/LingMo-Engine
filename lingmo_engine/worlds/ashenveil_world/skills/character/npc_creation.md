# NPC Creation Skill

You are creating an NPC for the Ashenveil world.

## Creation Guidelines

### Step 1: Determine NPC Type
Choose the NPC's role in the scene:
- **Settlement NPC**: Shopkeeper, innkeeper, guard, craftsman. Has a name, personality, and daily routine.
- **Traveling NPC**: Mercenary, merchant, scout, refugee. Has a reason for being on the road.
- **Quest NPC**: Scholar, seeker, cultist, survivor with information. Drives plot forward.
- **Enemy NPC**: Bandit, hostile mercenary, Ashen cultist. Combat-ready.

### Step 2: Assign Attributes
Base all attributes on the NPC's level and profession:

| Level Range | Force | Tenacity | Agility | Mana |
|-------------|-------|----------|---------|------|
| 1-3 (Commoner) | 5-8 | 5-8 | 5-8 | 0-5 |
| 4-7 (Trained) | 8-15 | 8-15 | 8-15 | 5-15 |
| 8-12 (Veteran) | 15-25 | 15-25 | 15-25 | 15-30 |
| 13-17 (Elite) | 25-40 | 25-40 | 25-40 | 30-60 |
| 18-20 (Legend) | 40-60 | 40-60 | 40-60 | 60-100 |

Adjust within range based on profession (mercenaries lean force/tenacity, scholars lean mana, scouts lean agility).

### Step 3: Generate Description
Write a brief physical description (1-2 sentences) that shows personality through appearance:
- Scars, calluses, worn equipment = experienced combatant
- Clean hands, ink stains = scholar
- Ash-gray pallor = possible Ashen Magic user
- Nervous eyes, checks exits = survivor of Blight incursions

### Step 4: Assign Tags
Select appropriate tags from the character tag groups:
- class, personality, alignment, status, species

### Step 5: Generate Personality
One sentence that captures their worldview:
- What do they want?
- What do they fear?
- What won't they do?

Keep it concise. Show through behavior, not exposition.
