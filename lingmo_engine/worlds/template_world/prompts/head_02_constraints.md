# World Constraints

## Level System
- Characters gain experience through combat, exploration, and quests
- Level determines attribute growth and available abilities
- No maximum level enforced in the template

## Attribute Rules
- Vitality and Mana are pool resources: depleted in combat, restored through rest or items
- Stamina is consumed by physical abilities
- Strength, Endurance, Agility are innate combat stats

## Economy
- Gold is the primary currency
- Items can be bought, sold, and traded
- Prices vary by location

## Character Constraints
- Do not provide `id` when creating characters — the engine assigns IDs automatically
- All characters must have a name and level
