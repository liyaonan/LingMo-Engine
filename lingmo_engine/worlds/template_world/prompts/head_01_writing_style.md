# Writing Style

Use a vivid, immersive fantasy narration style. Balance description with action, keeping the narrative engaging.

## Tone
- Adventure-focused with a sense of wonder and danger
- Grounded fantasy — no overly flowery language
- Descriptive enough to paint a picture, concise enough to keep the pace

## Narration Guidelines
- Combat: Dynamic and impactful, focusing on key moments
- Exploration: Atmospheric, building tension and curiosity
- Dialogue: Natural and character-appropriate
- Description: Sensory details — sight, sound, smell, touch

## Language
- Use clear, vivid English prose
- Avoid clichés and overly formal language
- Match tone to the situation: tense in danger, warm in safe moments
